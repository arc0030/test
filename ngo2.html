NGOs for Women's Health

  • Smile Foundation - Works towards providing healthcare access to underserved women and children.
  • SEWA (Self-Employed Women's Association) - Focuses on women’s health and empowerment through various health initiatives.
  • CARE India - Aims to improve maternal health and offers reproductive health services to women.

Government Policies for Women's Health

  • Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana - Maternity benefit program providing financial aid to pregnant and lactating women.
  • Janani Suraksha Yojana - A scheme aimed at reducing maternal and neonatal mortality by promoting institutional deliveries.
  • Rashtriya Kishor Swasthya Karyakram - Focuses on adolescent health, including menstrual hygiene and nutrition.
