Mental Health Resources

Support for Mental Well-being

At SakhiHealth, we prioritize your mental health. Explore our mental wellness tips, guides, and access our carefully curated support network to help you achieve balance and peace of mind.

Self-Care Tips

  • Mindfulness Practice: Spend 10-15 minutes each day focusing on your breathing. Mindfulness can reduce stress and increase overall well-being.
  • Exercise Regularly: Physical activity releases endorphins and can significantly boost your mood. Try activities like walking, yoga, or dancing.
  • Sleep Well: Ensure you get 7-9 hours of sleep each night. Consistent, quality sleep improves mental clarity and emotional regulation.
  • Limit Screen Time: Set boundaries for digital consumption. Reducing screen time, especially on social media, can alleviate anxiety.
  • Journaling: Writing down your thoughts can be a therapeutic exercise. Consider journaling to process your emotions and set daily goals.

Online Counseling Services

Access professional help from the comfort of your home. These platforms offer licensed therapists and counselors who specialize in women's mental health:

  • BetterHelp - Online counseling services with licensed therapists for general mental health support.
  • Talkspace - Provides therapy and psychiatry services with options for flexible communication (text, video, or audio).
  • 7 Cups - Connects individuals with trained listeners and licensed counselors for emotional support.

Self-Assessment Tools

Use these assessment tools to understand more about your mental health. These are not substitutes for professional advice but can help guide you:

  • Depression Screening - Evaluate if you may be experiencing symptoms of depression.
  • Anxiety Self-Test - Determine if you may have anxiety-related symptoms.
  • Stress Level Test - Assess your current stress levels and identify potential areas for improvement.

Community Support Networks

Connecting with others can offer emotional relief and build resilience. Here are communities and networks focused on supporting women’s mental health:

  • SupportGroups.com - Join groups based on specific mental health conditions and connect with others for mutual support.
  • Moments & Milestones - A support network for mothers dealing with mental health challenges and postpartum issues.
  • Women at the Refuge - A community for women overcoming mental health and trauma-related challenges.
